TfL Real-Time Lakehouse

Real-time London transport data on your laptop. Airflow ingests TfL arrivals and stores them in Parquet. dbt and DuckDB transform the data, Great Expectations validates it, and OpenLineage tracks data lineage — a fully observable, production-pattern lakehouse without cloud dependency.
